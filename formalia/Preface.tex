This project was built by seven engineering students from 3rd semester of Robotics at Aalborg University. The project was complemented by relevant courses intended to provide the knowledge and tools to actualise the project. Descriptions of the ideas and ways the solution was developed are presented with additional theoretical knowledge on various subjects to assist in comprehending them. A glossary has been made to provide the reader with explanations of the acronyms used throughout the project. Throughout the report, citations will be used to support certain statements, claims, or general theory. If the citation is placed within a sentence (before period), it supports only said sentence, and if the citation is placed at the end of a section (after period), it supports the entire section.

We would like to thank Aalborg University for supplying the CrustCrawler, additional resources and other necessary tools, as well as the relevant courses to complete this project. Additionally, we would like to thank our supervisor who proved very helpful, dedicated, and flexible throughout the making of this project. He provided much needed guidance and knowledge to assist us in the making of this project.
